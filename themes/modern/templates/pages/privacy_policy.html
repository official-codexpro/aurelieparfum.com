Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 1, 2025

At Aurélie Parfum, we recognize that privacy is a fundamental right. This Comprehensive Privacy Policy describes our practices regarding the collection, use, and protection of your data across all digital touchpoints.

Agreement Notice: By accessing our services, engaging with our boutique platform, or completing a purchase, you acknowledge that you have read and understood the terms of this policy.

01. Information We Collect

Personal Data Provided by You

  • Full legal name and title
  • Contact details (Personal email, mobile number)
  • Shipping, billing, and secondary addresses
  • Payment metadata (Tokenized card info)
  • Account credentials and fragrance preferences

Automated Digital Data

  • IP Geolocation and ISP details
  • Browser fingerprints and OS versions
  • Behavioral heatmaps and clickstream paths
  • Referral sources (Social media/Search engines)
  • Cookie-based session identifiers

02. Purpose of Data Processing

Your data allows us to provide a bespoke olfactory experience. We process information based on:

Fulfillment Managing logistics, payments, and returns.
Personalization Tailoring product recommendations to your taste.
Protection Detecting fraudulent transactions and bots.

03. Sharing & Disclosure

We only share data with vetted partners:

  • Logistics: BlueDart, FedEx, DHL
  • Finance: Stripe, Razorpay, PayPal
  • Analytics: Google Analytics, Hotjar

No Sale Policy: We never trade your data for profit.

04. Technical Safeguards

  • End-to-End SSL/TLS Encryption
  • PCI-DSS Compliant Payment Gates
  • Encrypted Database Storage (AES-256)
  • Biometric Access for Server Personnel
  • Quarterly Vulnerability Assessments

05. Cookie Policy

Cookies help us remember your "Fragrance Cart" and login state. You may opt-out via browser settings, though some luxury features may be restricted.

06. Your Legal Rights

Under global privacy laws (GDPR/CCPA/DPDP), you maintain the right to Access, Rectify, Delete, or Export your data at any time.
