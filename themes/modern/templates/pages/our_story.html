Our Story

The Journey of Making Luxury Accessible

01. The Beginning

Aurélie Parfum was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to make luxury fragrances accessible to every Indian who appreciates the art of perfumery. Our founder noticed a significant gap in the Indian market while traveling.

Premium fragrances were either prohibitively expensive or of questionable quality. There had to be a better way – a way to bring authentic, high-quality fragrances to Indian consumers without the unnecessary luxury markup.

02. The Journey

In 2020, we started Aurélie Parfum with a small team of passionate perfume enthusiasts. We partnered with experienced perfumers and sourced premium ingredients from around the world.

The first year was spent testing hundreds of formulations to ensure they lasted in the Indian climate. Today, we are proud to have served 50,000+ happy customers across the country.
