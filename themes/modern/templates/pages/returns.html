Returns & Refunds

Our Quality Assurance Guarantee

🔄

7-Day Concierge Return Policy

Seamless returns for a worry-free fragrance experience.

Eligibility & Criteria

✅ Valid Return Cases

  • Damaged or defective transit handling
  • Incorrect product dispatch
  • Tampered security seals upon arrival
  • Incomplete order components
  • Significant variance from description

📋 Essential Conditions

  • Product must be strictly unused
  • Original luxury packaging remains intact
  • All batch tags and labels attached
  • Proof of purchase (Invoice) included

Hygiene Protocol: Due to the artisanal nature of our fragrances, products with broken atomizers or "tested" liquids cannot be returned for hygiene and safety reasons, unless verified as defective.

The Return Journey

01. Initiate Request

Contact support via support@aurelieparfum.com within 7 days. Include high-resolution images if the item is damaged.

02. Professional Review

Our quality control team reviews your request within 24-48 hours. Approval is sent via a Confirmation Ticket.

03. Secure Packaging

Carefully place the item in its original box. We recommend using the outer shipping box provided during delivery for maximum safety.

04. Complimentary Pickup

Our logistics partner will arrive for a doorstep pickup at no cost to you. Please ensure the Confirmation Ticket is ready.

💰 Refund Logistics

Inspection: 1-2 Business Days
Initiation: 2-3 Business Days
Settlement: 5-7 Business Days

Refunds are credited back to the original payment source (UPI, Credit/Debit Card, or Net Banking).

🔄 Exchange & ❌ Cancel

Exchanges: We follow a "Return & Re-order" model to ensure you get your desired fragrance as quickly as possible without manual processing delays.

Cancellations: Permitted via your dashboard only before dispatch. Post-dispatch, please refuse the delivery or follow the return process.
